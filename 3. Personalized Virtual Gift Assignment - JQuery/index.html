Gift Section

Welcome again to The Holly Bough. This year, as well as providing local finance for local causes, we've teamed up with a few of Cork's more distinguished PRCer's , who are showing their charitable side by assisting some of the causes. Depending on the amount donated, you will receive or be entered into a draw for the following :

  • The Frank and Walters free download of their cover of "I saw Santa kissing Grandma" (all donations €2 or over receive this).
  • That '80's Agony Aunt, Terence, coming to your local pub to cure all your ails (all donations €5 or over to be entered into a draw).
  • A training session at the Southern San Siro, Turners Cross, with John Caulfield (all donations €15 or over to be entered into a draw).
  • A training session with Derval O' Rourke in the Mardyke (all donations €20 or over to be entered into a draw)
  • A certain Cobh resident (who is very "Keane") to accompany yourself and two others to the first Cork City Home fixture.
